How the MSR Program Works:

Earn Rebates with Every Purchase: Enjoy a 5% rebate on every fully-paid order (excluding shipping and late charges). Watch how your rewards grow with each transaction.

Transparent Tracking: Your rebates will be meticulously recorded in your enclosed company account details by Idol Memory. These records will be reviewed quarterly, providing you with a clear overview of your available and pending rebates.

Seamless Redemption: Once your rebates are available after a waiting period of 3 months from the order delivery date, you can apply them towards your new purchases. It's a simple way to save on your future orders.

Exclusively for Future Purchases: While the rebates are non-redeemable as cash, they are a powerful tool to offset costs of your upcoming orders from Idol Memory. Your loyalty is truly rewarded seamlessly.

Eligibility:

Current Customers: If your orders from Idol Memory have totaled $30,000 or more within the trailing 12 months (FYTD), you are automatically eligible for the MSR Program. There are no additional annual fees for you to join! We simply need your approval to enroll you in MSR Program.

Bonus for Rebates: For eligible distributors, we have even more good news! You will receive wholesale rebates to orders made in last 3 months.

New Customers: New to Idol Memory? When your annual revenue reaches $250,000, you qualify for this program. A nominal annual fee of $150 applies, but the benefits are substantial. Once you have a $10,000 (net-invoice) with Idol Memory after this waiting period, you can start applying your rewards to future orders.
